Watermelon Slush

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Cubed seeded watermelon
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons sweetened strawberry lemonade drink mix

Steps

  1. Place the watermelon pieces onto a placstic-wrapped baking sheet, and freeze until

    solid, about 45 minutes

  2. Place the frozen watermelon, water, and strawberry lemonade drink mix in a blender

    blend until slushy

  3. Serve in a Margherita glass
