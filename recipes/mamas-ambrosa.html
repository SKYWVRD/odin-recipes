Mama's Ambrosia

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans manderin orange segments, drained
  • 2 cans fruit cocktail, drained
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 cups minature marshmellows
  • 2 cups flaked coconut
  • 1 container sour cream

Steps

  1. Mix together oranges, fruit coctail, walnuts, marshmellows, and coconut in a large bowl

  2. Fold in the sour cream

  3. Let mixture chill for at least an hour

  4. Stir again before serving
